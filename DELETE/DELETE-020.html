Test Case DELETE-020
Title Pressing "Delete", when there is nothing on the display does nothing
Objective

This test case is intended to verify the following implied or specified requirements:

  • When the delete button is pressed, rtnv shall do nothing if the ticker field is empty.

This test case verifies that pressing the delete key when the ticker field is null does nothing.
Setup
Actions
  1. Delete the app from the target device.
  2. Compile and load rtnv onto the target Android device.
  3. Verify the compile completes with no errors.
  4. Verify the installation completes with no errors.
  5. Select the "Ticker Symbol" field and enter the ticker value of AAPL.
  6. Select the "Number of Shares" field and enter the value 200.
  7. Press the "Enter" button.
  8. Select the "Ticker Symbol" field and enter the ticker value of INTC.
  9. Select the "Number of Shares" field and enter the value 500.
  10. Press the "Enter" button.
  11. Press the "Next" button and verify that the symbol AAPL is displayed with 200 shares and that a non zero value is displayed for the last closing price of the stock.
  12. Press the "Delete" button and verify that the screen is "blanked".
  13. Press the "Next" button and verify that the symbol INTC is displayed with 500 shares and that a non zero value is displayed for the last closing price of the stock.
  14. Press the "Delete" button and verify that the screen is "blanked".
  15. Press the "Delete" button and verify that the screen remains the same.
  16. Select the "Ticker Symbol" field and enter the ticker value of AAPL.
  17. Select the "Number of Shares" field and enter the value 200.
  18. Press the "Enter" button.
  19. Select the "Ticker Symbol" field and enter the ticker value of INTC.
  20. Select the "Number of Shares" field and enter the value 500.
  21. Press the "Enter" button.
  22. Press the "Delete" button (with the field null).
  23. Press the "Next" button and verify that the symbol AAPL is displayed with 200 shares and that a non zero value is displayed for the last closing price of the stock.
  24. Press the "Delete" button and verify that the screen is "blanked".
  25. Press the "Next" button and verify that the symbol INTC is displayed with 500 shares and that a non zero value is displayed for the last closing price of the stock.
  26. Press the "Delete" button and verify that the screen is "blanked".
  27. Press the "Delete" button and verify that the screen remains the same.
Expected If the ticker symbol field is null, pressing the delete button will do nothing.
Results PASSED
Cleanup
  • Unnecessary
Requirements RTNV-PR-DELETE-020
